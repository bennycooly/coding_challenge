Go to the Calendar, and click on a day on the calendar. You will be presented with a list of events. Click on one of the links to learn more about the event. Click the "Sign Up!" button at the bottom of the screen.
If the event is an internal app event, it will automatically be added to Your Events.
If the event is an external event, there will be a disclaimer. The Submit button at the bottom of the page will take you to the official website where you can sign up for the event. Clicking the Submit button will also add the event to Your Events.
