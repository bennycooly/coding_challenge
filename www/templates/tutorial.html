Welcome Tutorial

Welcome!

This is CollaborAT&T, your home for serving the community. Let's get started!

Swipe left or tap the "next" button to continue.

Your Home

Here is your home page. It will display events you're signed up for, upcoming service events, and fundraisers that are in progress.

Your Action Menu

Tap the circular icon to open your quick menu. Here you have access to the most frequently used parts of the app all in one place!

Your Profile

Your profile page will give you a summary of your service achievements and history. You can view your information, events you've created, and your service progress.

Service Calendar

This is the service calendar for all events and fundraisers. Days marked with blue indicate service events and new fundraisers. Clicking on other days will show fundraisers that are currently in progress. To create an event, just tap the icon on the upper right hand corner!

Settings

This is your settings page. Here you can edit your profile, manage your event notifications, read about frequently asked questions, and more!

Finish

Thanks for viewing the CollaborAT&T tutorial! If you ever need to access this again, you can open the tutorial from the settings menu.
